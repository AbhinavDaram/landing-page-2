      Gary

      January 2024 - April 2024

      This was done at the Controls Department Lead on FRC 8728, Troy Argonauts for the 2024 FRC Season: Crescendo. Under this role, I led all the members of the controls subgroup, giving out projects to students who needed as well as program the bot as well. Under this role I also experimented with datalogging, which allowed me to log important information such as the amount of power drawn by a motor which can be used to solve potential issues. In fact, this was used to identify a major issue where we found out the robot was underperforming, allowing us to put current limits so that it would perform as intended. All programming work, from sensors to main subsystems where checked and edited by me as well as some were written by me last second during competitions to make sure everything was running smoothly and any previous issues would be resolved. I was also a member of the drive team for this robot, under the operator role where I would control all the subsystems other than the chassis. I controlled the arm, maniuplator, and climber. Alongside this I was also assisting with any fabrication or electrical tasks assisting with building subsystems or wiring them up to the robot.
